The Brief

On the matter of Balatro.

Balatro presents itself as a card game, and in this it is being modest. You are dealt a hand and asked to assemble a respectable poker hand. The usual company: a pair, a flush, and so on.

Thereafter the proceedings turn irregular. Cards known as Jokers begin rewriting the rules to suit you, until a humble pair of twos is persuaded to pay out like a sound pension.

There are no opponents to face and nothing to subscribe to. The Parlour asks only that you not regard “one more round” as a binding estimate.

♠
Why this one, for you

The selection was deliberate.

Your file describes a gentleman who enjoys the occasional hand of cards and otherwise leaves games well alone. This was not treated as an obstacle. It was the brief.

Balatro belongs to a category called the roguelike: the preferred genre of the two people who commissioned this service, and one usually fenced off behind a great deal of jargon. Balatro keeps the jargon to itself and asks only that you know which hand beats which. You already do. The Parlour can imagine no gentler way into the family habit.

Your concierge service

Delivered in person.

The game resides in a digital establishment. The Parlour will see to the formalities. You need lift nothing heavier than a playing card.

  1. I

    An account, opened

    Balatro is kept at a reputable shop called Steam. An account will be opened in your name, at no charge, in the time it takes to pour a drink.

  2. II

    The gift, settled

    The game is placed directly in your account, paid in full, and left waiting in your library.

  3. III

    Attendance in person

    Your concierge calls on you personally to see everything installed and running. You are asked only to be present, and ideally seated.

What happens next.

Your concierge will present himself in person to complete the arrangements. No preparation is required on your part. You may wish to set the evening aside; you will not be needing the rest of it.
